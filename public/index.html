What is The Gay Agenda?

The Gay Agenda is a monthly queer comedy showcase that started in March of this year. Highlighting queer comedians, artists, and also prioritizing bringing the community together to create a discourse about LBGTQ+ history, along with sexual health, positivity, and education. But our top priority is having a good, gay time -- always hosted by Emmet Claire.
